--- layout: home title: River Hill SciOly Invitational subtitle: February 28th - March 6th, 2021 --- Welcome to the first annual River Hill Division C Science Olympiad Invitational! River Hill Science Olympiad is a completely free invitational that caters to the national field of underserved schools by offering free opportunities to compete. Scheduled before many regionals and state competitions, RHSOI will give teams a chance to practice in a competitive environment before their upcoming local competitions. All tests will be written by experienced Division C competitors and Science Olympiad alumni from across the country.
